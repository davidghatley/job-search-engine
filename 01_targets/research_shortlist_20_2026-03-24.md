3. **Automattic** — Focus on remote async execution, analytics, and internal platform leverage.

6. **Workato** — Emphasize iPaaS orchestration, business systems integration, and workflow impact metrics.
7. **Retool** — Position as internal tools builder with rapid admin app delivery and measurable ops efficiency.

9. **Cloudflare** — Focus only on clearly commercial/non-clearance teams; stress platform + automation strength.
10. **Stripe** — Emphasize internal systems and analytics workflows that support revenue operations.
11. **Shopify** — Lead with scale-ready data workflows and internal operations tooling.
12. **PostHog** — Position around product analytics pipelines, event quality, and experimentation support.

### Albuquerque / NM local-hybrid

## Guardrails While Executing
- Re-check each posting for **security clearance**, **eligibility to obtain clearance**, **ITAR/EAR**, or **classified work** wording before applying.
- For public sector roles, verify role scope stays in non-defense/civil operations.
- Prioritize openings where outcomes can be framed in terms of time saved, reliability increased, or decision speed improved.
